## Visual Theme
A high-contrast, clinical-editorial aesthetic suited for elite security advisory. Crisp layout structures, architectural grid foundations, and calm, confident digital color accents communicate unmatched operational resilience.

## Art Direction
- Concept: An architectural blueprint for enterprise security and privacy consulting.
- Signature move: Micro-grid border separators mapping sections into clean, technical modules.
- Layout rhythm: Dense but structured data segments interspersed with expansive, high-impact editorial column blocks.

## Typography
- Scale: editorial
- Headlines: Plus Jakarta Sans — geometric, authoritative, engineered sans with tight tracking.
- Body: Inter — highly readable, neutral, clean Swiss sans.

## Components
- Buttons: Sharp rectangular corners (radius-sm), flat solid fills, high contrast hover animations (slight scale and color shift).
- Cards: Minimalist border-based containment, pure white surfaces, no shadows, thin `divide-y divide-border` separators inside.
- Sections: Alternating between pure clinical light bases and authoritative deep-navy dark blocks to delineate strategic shifts.
- Imagery: High-tech consulting, real human interactions in executive settings, modern architecture, real cyber-operations rooms. Cold-toned, corporate documentary photography.

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO use thin border grids to partition structural columns.
- DO keep copy direct, authoritative, and focused on risk-to-opportunity transformation.
- DON'T use cartoon security icons, digital shields, or generic glowing-padlock stock art.
- DON'T allow text to span wider than its natural column boundaries; maintain strict horizontal track minima.
